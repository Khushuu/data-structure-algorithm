2ï¸âƒ£ Adjacency List

Each vertex stores a list of adjacent vertices (using vectors, linked lists, or maps).

// Adding edges (undirected graph)

Add Edge	: O(1)
Remove Edge	: O(degree of node)
Check Edge Exists:	O(degree of node)
Space Complexity:	O(N + E)

ğŸ”¹ Best for: Sparse graphs (saves space).
ğŸ”¹ Not good for: Fully connected graphs (slightly slower edge lookup than matrix).
